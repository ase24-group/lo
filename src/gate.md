## Installation

Grab the source code:

    curl https://raw.githubusercontent.com/timm/lo/6jan24/src/gate.lua

    curl https://github.com/timm/lo/blob/6jan24/data/x

where `x` is one of 
auto93.csv,
china.csv,
coc1000.csv,
